Predict

PON-Sol2 is a fast predictor based on protein FASTA sequences to identify amino acid substitutions that increase, decrease of have no effect on the protein solubility. To start using PON-Sol2, you just need to provide protein FASTA sequences and amino acid substitutions. For protein FASTA sequences, there are 2 input ways: directly input FASTA sequences or input protein IDs(GI, Ensemble ID or UniProt). You can directly provide the corresponding content or upload files.
