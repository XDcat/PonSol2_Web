Wecome to PON-Sol2

Solubility is a central protein property and when reduced can lead to diseases. We have developed a prediction method, PON-Sol2, to identify amino acid substitutions that increase, decrease of have no effect on the protein solubility.

Input Format

There are two input ways that have different input formats. Faste sequences and amino acid substitutions are provided seperatly, or IDs and amino acid substitutions are put together.

Dataset

Altogether 11201 amino acid substitutions in 77 proteins are collected. After filtering, 5666 variants are used for training, and 662 variants are used for blind testing.
