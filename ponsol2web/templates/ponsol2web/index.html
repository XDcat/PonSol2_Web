Wecome to PON-Sol2

Solubility is a central protein property and when reduced can lead to diseases. PON-Sol2 is a prediction program to detect amino acid substitutions that increase, decrease or have no effect on the protein solubility. The method is based on a gradient boosting algorithm and it has been trained on a large data set. The method is fast and has high performance. It can be used to predict variations for proteins from any organism. You can also visit the mirror website on Soochow University, China.
